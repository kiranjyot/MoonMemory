## GW Neutrino Models

This directory contains GW neutrino models from two publications:

1. Vartanyan et al 2023; https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2023PhRvD.107j3015V/abstract
2. Choi et al 2024; https://ui.adsabs.harvard.edu/abs/2024arXiv240801525C/abstract
